## Update on Crypto Regulation

It's been a busy year for crypto regulation, but it feel as though this week was especially crazy. 
The search for Terraform Labs CEO Do Kwon continues as the SEC formally charges Terraform of fraud. In his statement, SEC chair Gary Gensler said "Terraform and Kwon repeatedly misled and deceived investors that a popular Korean mobile payment application used the Terra blockchain to settle transactions that would accrue value to LUNA"([source](https://www.sec.gov/news/press-release/2023-32#:~:text=%22We%20allege%20that%20Terraform%20and,said%20SEC%20Chair%20Gary%20Gensler.)), among other claims surroudning LUNA and Terra USD.
